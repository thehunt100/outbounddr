FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is OutboundDR?

    OutboundDR is a human-powered LinkedIn Lead Generation service. This means we use no automation tools whatsoever to generate leads for you. Everything is performed by our team.

  • Why not use automation tools?

    The automation tools come with many issues, like profiles being permanently blocked because LinkedIn recognizes non-human activity.

  • How does OutboundDR work?

    Once you are signed up, you get a free access to your custom Dashboard. In the Dashboard can create a campaign and add credits to it and click start, it’s that simple. Each activity is "paid" with the credits.

  • How much does it cost?

    You can calculate the exact price of your campaign in the calculator in your Dashboard. You can also access the calculator here.

  • What Linkedin activity can be automated using OutboundDR?

    You can automate your connection requests, messages, profile views. We can also offer custom activity automation, ask us about it.

  • Do I need LinkedIn Sales Navigator?

    Sales navigator is not required but it greatly improves your ability to target your ideal customer. It also removes the LinkedIn restrictions for browsing people.
